Diagnosing

Autism Diagnosis in Utah – No Waitlist Evaluations at Kuska

Wondering if you or your child might be on the autism spectrum?

If you or your child has difficulty with communication, social interaction, or behavior, a professional evaluation can provide the answers you need and help you access supportive services.

Why Getting a Diagnosis
Matters

  • Understand your or your child's strengths and challenges.
  • Access ABA therapy and insurance-covered support.
  • Start early intervention for better outcomes.

What To Expect?

1. Clinical Interview/Questionnaire

2. Professional Observation and
Testing

3. Clear Diagnosis & Report

4. Connection to ABA Therapy
