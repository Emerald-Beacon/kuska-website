ABA Programs

A partner in your journey, with therapy plans that fit real family life.

Kuska provides personalized ABA therapy in Utah through in-home, in-clinic, and hybrid models designed around each child’s goals, availability, and learning style.

Comprehensive ABA

Typically 30 to 35 hours per week for children who benefit from broad, consistent support across communication, behavior, social skills, and daily living.

Focused ABA

Often 15 to 25 hours per week for more targeted goals, such as reducing a challenging behavior, improving a key social skill, or building independence.
