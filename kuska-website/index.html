Welcome to Kuska Autism Services

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential.

At Kuska Autism Services, we provide comprehensive Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy tailored to each child's unique needs. Our team of Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and experienced therapists work closely with families to create individualized treatment plans that promote meaningful progress.

With locations in Bountiful and Draper, Utah, we're committed to making quality autism services accessible to families throughout the region.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ABA therapy?

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is a scientific, evidence-based approach to understanding behavior and how it is affected by the environment. ABA therapy focuses on improving specific behaviors and skills through positive reinforcement and systematic teaching methods.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Contact us through our form or by phone to schedule an initial consultation. We'll discuss your child's needs, verify insurance benefits, and begin the assessment process to create a personalized treatment plan.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers including Medicaid. Our billing team will verify your benefits and help you understand your coverage before services begin.

What ages do you serve?

We provide services to children of all ages, from early intervention for toddlers through adolescence. Our programs are tailored to meet each child's developmental stage and individual needs.
