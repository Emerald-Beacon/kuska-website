Why Choose Us?

Locally Owned & Operated

We are dedicated to supporting families affected by autism spectrum disorder (ASD) in our community. Our team of highly educated and compassionate therapists is committed to providing personalized ABA therapy services that meet the unique needs and strengths of each individual. We understand that every family’s journey with autism is different, which is why we offer a wide range of services that can be customized to meet each family’s specific needs. Our goal is to empower individuals with ASD to achieve greater independence, improve their quality of life, and reach their full potential.

How It Works

1 Contact Us

On our initial call, we will discuss your current challenges and answer any questions you have. We will learn more about you and share about Kuska’s approach to supporting families.

2 Assessment

During this part of the process, we will conduct an assessment using direct and indirect methods. We will look to determine medical necessity, program placement, identify strengths and areas of need.

3 Collaborate

Collaboration is pivotal to your loved one’s success. We work with familes and caregivers to identify goals and creating a roadmap to reach them.
